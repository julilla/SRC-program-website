Socially Responsible Computing Teaching Assistants (STAs)

Socially Responsible Computing Teaching Assistants (STAs) are embedded in a range of CS courses each semester to help develop and implement content on the social impact of technology and tailor it to the technical content of each course.

In Spring 2024, our team of 18 STAs is supporting 11 courses.

  • CSCI0111: Naemi Ditiatkovsky, Kawehi Kelii
  • CSCI0220: Grant Landon, Samantha Shulman
  • CSCI0300: Doren Hsiao-Wecksler, Alex Wick
  • CSCI0320: Gabi Becher, Anna Hurd
  • CSCI1420: Emma Huang, Julie Qian
  • CSCI1430: Allen Dufort, Connor Flick
